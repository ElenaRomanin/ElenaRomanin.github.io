Welcome!

I am Elena Romanin, a PhD student in Political and Social Science at the Pompeu Fabra University (UPF), Barcelona. In my research, I explore the determinants of people’s support for, or rejection to, climate change policies and related behaviours, primarily using survey data and experiments. I am currently part of the research team of the ATT Climate Research Project funded by the Spanish Ministry of Science and Innovation.
